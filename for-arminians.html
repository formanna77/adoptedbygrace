You Love Jesus. You're Just Missing Something.

You've been taught that your decision saved you. What if the Bible says something different — something better? Walk with us. No ambush. No condescension. Just Scripture, logic, and a question you've never been asked.

6 Steps
1 Question That Changes Everything

We're not going to tell you what to think. We're going to ask you a question — and then walk through the Bible's answer together, one step at a time. You already believe most of what's coming. You just haven't followed the thread to its conclusion yet. If what we show you is wrong, you'll know it by the end. And if it's right — you'll feel something you've never felt before: the weight of trying to earn grace finally lifting off your shoulders.
