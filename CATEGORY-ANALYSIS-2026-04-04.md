**Current State:**
- Questions: 48 pages (scripture proofs + objection answers + theology questions)
- Objections Answered: 18 pages (emotional/philosophical responses)

```

**Examples of Semantic Duplication:**
1. Q: "Is God Unfair? – Romans 9:19-23" vs O: "Is Election Fair? Why Grace Is Unfair"
2. Q: "Doesn't God Want Everyone Saved?" vs O: "Doesn't God Want All People Saved?"
3. Q: "Is Free Will Real?" vs O: "Isn't Faith a Choice?"

**The Real Problem:** No clear architecture distinguishing:
- Scripture-first proofs (should stay in Questions)
- Emotional/objection handling (should route to Healing)

**Recommendation for Reorganization:**
```
Current: Questions (48) + Objections (18) = 66 pages doing overlapping work
Future: Questions (35-40) = scripture proofs only
        → Objections (18) = emotional responses to hard truths
        → Philosophy (8) = logical proofs

```

## PART 3: THE TWO ARMS IN CURRENT STRUCTURE

### How "Sword & Balm" Should Work

**According to project instructions:**
> "Every demolition page MUST end with a doorway to a devotion page.
> Demolition without devotion = despair.
> Devotion without demolition = complacency.
> Both together = transformation."

**Current Implementation:**

```
DEMOLITION ZONE (26 pages)
  ↓ should link to
DEVOTIONALS (18 pages) + HEALING (29 pages)
  
PLUS SUPPORTING:
  Why We Resist (20) = explaining why they believe the lie
  Philosophy (8) = proving truth philosophically
  Theology (20) = complete framework
```

**The Problem:** The links between categories aren't automatic. A reader hitting "Demolition: Romans 10:9-10" doesn't know to look for comfort in "Devotional: My Chains Fell Away."

**Architecture Solution:**
1. Every Demolition page should end with: "Now that you see the lie, here's comfort in the truth:" → [Devotional link]
2. Every hard Question should route to: "Feel attacked? Here's why:" → [Psychology page from Why We Resist]
3. Every Objection page should offer: "Rest here:" → [Healing category page]

```
Pain Acknowledged                    Grace Applied
───────────────────────────────────  ───────────────────────────────────
Anxious Mind (fear/control)   ────→  "God's Sovereignty of Sleep"
Broken Mirror (identity)      ────→  "You Were Created for Mercy"

                    ────→  Philosophy (why the lie appeals)
