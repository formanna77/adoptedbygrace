The Shattered Lens

We live in the first era where truth itself is under assault. Deepfakes. AI. Political spin. Misinformation. Romans 1 predicted this: when a culture suppresses truth, truth becomes unrecognizable. The sovereignty of God is the only stable ground in a world where every other floor is shifting.

John 14:6 — "I am the way, the truth, and the life."

In a world where nothing feels certain, the certainty of a sovereign God who controls all things is the most relevant doctrine alive. This is not theology for quiet contemplation. This is theology for surviving the crisis of our age.

"For the wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness and unrighteousness of men, who by their unrighteousness suppress the truth. For what can be known about God is plain to them, because God has shown it to them." — Romans 1:18-19 (ESV)
