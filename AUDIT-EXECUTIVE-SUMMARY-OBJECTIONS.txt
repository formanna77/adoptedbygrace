Date: 2026-04-04 | Pages Analyzed: 25 | Issues Found: 39

MISSION VIOLATION
These pages are meant to "destroy the insidious lie" and "leave zero escape 
routes." Instead, several contain language that PRESERVES escape routes by:
- Using "paradox" as a conclusion rather than resolving it
- Hedging with "maybe," "perhaps," and "some believe"
- Validating objections without sufficient redirection
- Conceding to human autonomy rather than demolishing it

================================================================================
CRITICAL ISSUES (4)
================================================================================

1. PARADOX AS CLOSURE (4 pages)
   Files: objection-faith-choice.html, objection-why-command.html,
           objection-god-could-save-all.html, question-was-christ-a-robot.html
   
   Problem: Pages end with "this is a paradox" rather than resolving it.
   Impact: Smart objectors hear "you've proven my point—it's incoherent."
   
   Examples:
   - objection-faith-choice.html line 669: "Keller captures the paradox perfectly"
   - objection-god-could-save-all.html line 259: "Live in the paradox"
   - objection-why-command.html line 380: Asserts but doesn't explain the mechanism
   
   Fix: Replace all paradox language with EXPLANATION of why these aren't 
        actually contradictory. Show the mechanism, not the mystery.

2. "SECONDARY ISSUE" FRAMING (1 page)
   File: objection-faith-choice.html line 748
   
   Problem: Sets up "maybe this is a secondary issue" - violates CLAUDE.md mandate.
   Impact: Plants doubt about Reformed consensus.
   Impact: This IS the central issue (grace vs. works).
   
   Fix: Remove the "maybe." Say: "Objectors claim this is secondary. They're wrong."

3. "LIVE IN THE PARADOX" DODGE (1 page)
   File: objection-god-could-save-all.html line 259
   
   Problem: Tells reader to accept contradiction without explanation.
   Impact: Fails to demolish the objection—just asks reader to tolerate incoherence.
   
   Fix: Explain the mechanism. How does decree accomplish itself through command?
        Not "live with it"—understand it.

MAJOR ISSUES (18+)
================================================================================

WEAK QUALIFIERS THAT UNDERMINE CERTAINTY
   Files: objection-fairness.html, objection-why-evangelize.html, 
           question-freewill.html, question-godwants.html, objection-god-could-save-all.html
   
   Instances: 15+ uses of "perhaps," "maybe," "some believe," "arguably"
   
   Examples:
   - "The tension remains" (objection-fairness.html line 336)
   - "Perhaps the strongest text" (question-godwants.html line 288)
   - "Maybe God's grace precedes our coming to faith" (question-freewill.html line 826)
   
   Problem: These hedge on certainty when Scripture is UNAMBIGUOUS.
   Fix: Replace with confident assertions. Use Scripture as the final word.

FREE WILL LANGUAGE THAT PRESERVES AUTONOMY
   Files: objection-robots.html, objection-responsibility.html, 
           objection-faith-choice.html, question-freewill.html
   
   Problem: Pages argue AGAINST libertarian free will while using "free will" 
            language positively. Headers like "Free Will Is..." without the negation.
   
   Fix: Reframe all headers from "Free Will" to "The Libertarian Free Will Objection"
        Make clear: libertarian free will is incoherent, not just misunderstood.

INCOMPLETE RESPONSES TO "BUT WHAT ABOUT..."
   Problem: Some objections are acknowledged but not fully destroyed.
   
   Examples:
   - "Isn't faith a response?" (objection-faith-choice.html) - doesn't explain why
     "response" doesn't restore human agency
   - "How is free will prepared?" (question-faithgift.html) - doesn't define "prepared"
   - "Don't I have free will?" (objection-why-believe.html) - pivots instead of answering
   
   Fix: Answer every "but what about" fully. Close every escape route.

FALSE ALTERNATIVES PRESENTED WITH HEDGES
   Files: objection-god-could-save-all.html (Molinism), question-freewill.html (prevenient grace)
   
   Problem: These are presented as live options ("some try," "maybe") rather than
            demolished as incoherent.
   
   Fix: Expand objection sections to show why Molinism is incoherent,
        why prevenient grace has zero exegetical foundation.

EMOTIONAL VALIDATION WITHOUT REDIRECTION
   Files: objection-love.html, objection-why-command.html
   
   Problem: Validates objection's emotional force but doesn't quickly redirect to
            God's glory and beauty.
   
   Example: objection-love.html line 398 validating the feeling for too long
   
   Fix: Empathize faster, pivot faster to why the feeling is wrong and what 
        God's election ACTUALLY means for His glory.
