- Lead with "Scripture teaches," NEVER labels.
- Brilliant connections: 2,000-year-old letters perfectly describing phenomena modern science took centuries to discover. That creates awe.
- Warm, not weaponized. Not "here's why you're blind" but "here's why this is hard for all of us."
- Name specific researchers and studies. Distinguish established findings from controversial claims.
