The Doctrines of Grace

Flagship Essay

Soli Deo Gloria

The five points of Calvinism are not five separate doctrines. They are a single argument — and if you grant the first point, the other four follow with the force of gravity. This essay walks through 35+ passages to demonstrate that Total Depravity necessitates Unconditional Election, which necessitates Limited Atonement, which necessitates Irresistible Grace, which guarantees the Perseverance of the Saints. Remove any link and the chain collapses. Keep them all and the gospel holds.
