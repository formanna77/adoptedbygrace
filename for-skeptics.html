You Came Here Hunting for Intellectual Honesty

You don't believe — or you're not sure. That's not stupid. That's a starting point.

Here's the deal. Most religious websites will try to sell you something warm and fuzzy. We won't. This site makes a specific, falsifiable claim: that the universe — including your thoughts, your doubts, and the fact that you're reading this right now — operates under a sovereignty so total that even your skepticism was anticipated before you were born. We're not asking you to feel something. We're asking you to follow the evidence. If we're wrong, you'll know. If we're right, you won't be able to unknow it.

This path is designed for the intellectually rigorous. No appeals to emotion. No "just believe." Each step builds on the last. Take them in order, or jump to whatever catches your eye. We're not afraid of your questions — we built this site for them.
