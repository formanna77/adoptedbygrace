## HOW THIS QUEUE WAS RANKED

Four weights combined into the rank:

1. **Inbound links** — from `strategic-audit.js`. The more pages that lean on this page, the more a rewrite raises the water level of the whole site.
2. **Doctrinal centrality** — does the page carry a doctrine of grace load-bearing to the site's mission? (Total depravity and unconditional election weighted heaviest, since the Linchpin argument flows through them.)
3. **Category weight** — questions, devotionals, and systematic pages carry higher weight than comparison / history pages, because they are where the reader converts.
4. **Rescue potential** — honest estimate: how many readers has this page likely rescued already, and how many more if it is lifted to VOICE.md standard?

Pages already at masterpiece standard (the eleven pages quarried for VOICE.md itself) are **not** in this queue — they are the floor the rewrites must clear. They will receive tuning passes, not rewrites.

## EXECUTION PROTOCOL

### The Cadence

One spine page per session until the Spine (Tier S, #1–10) is complete. Minimum one full hour per rewrite. No parallel work during a spine rewrite — the writing bench is the only station open.

### The Proof-of-Concept

The first spine rewrite under VOICE.md is `question-depravity.html` — the Linchpin. The rewrite is produced this session as the proof-of-concept that VOICE.md is enforceable.

### The Validation Gate

Every rewrite passes, in order:

1. VOICE.md Section XI checklist, item by item.
2. `node build-search-index.js` — success.
3. `node build-mega-menu.js` — success.
4. `node build-homepage-counts.js` — success.
5. `node auto-linker.js` — success.
6. `node wire-orphans.js` — success.
7. `node validate-site.js` — no errors flagged for the rewritten file.

Any failure on any step sends the rewrite back to the bench.

### The Rule Against Drift

No rewrite is shipped until the writer can point — with their finger, not rhetorically — to the Sentence of the Page. If the sentence is not there, the rewrite is not done.

### The Measure

Not word count. Not page count. Not SEO rank. Did a reader find God here? Did a heart that was hiding stop hiding? Did someone who believed they were holding on discover they were held?
