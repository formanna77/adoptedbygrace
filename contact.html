Write to Us

Whether you have a question about Scripture that you can't shake, want to share the moment God opened your eyes to His sovereign grace, or simply want to push back on something you read here — we're listening. Every message matters.

"Before I formed you in the womb I knew you, before you were born I set you apart."

JEREMIAH 1:5

foreknownone@adoptedbygrace.net

What We Love to Hear

Your testimony. Did God open your eyes to His sovereignty? Did a page on this site play a part? Those stories are fuel for this ministry. They remind us why every late night was worth it.

Your hardest questions. The ones your pastor dodges. The ones that keep you up at night. If we haven't addressed it yet, your question might become the next article. Check our Questions section — your answer may already be there.

Your honest pushback. If you think we've gotten something wrong, tell us. We steel-man every objection — including the ones aimed at us. Iron sharpens iron.

What to Expect

We will do our best to respond to every message. This is a small ministry powered by conviction, not a staff — so responses may take a few days. But know this: your message will be read, prayed over, and valued.

If you're in the middle of a crisis of faith — if the ground just disappeared beneath you and you don't know what to believe anymore — please don't suffer alone. Reach out, and let's talk. That's exactly the moment this site was built for. You might want to start with Start Here or rest in a devotional while you wait.
