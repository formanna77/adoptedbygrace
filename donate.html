Support This Ministry

This site will always be free. But it isn't free to run.

What Your Support Makes Possible

Adopted by Grace exists for one reason: to reach the people God is drawing to Himself and give them the clearest, most beautiful, most devastating presentation of His sovereign grace the internet has ever seen. Every page, every article, every argument — built for the person Googling at 2am who can't shake the feeling that something doesn't add up.

This is not a church. There is no staff. There is no budget committee. There is one person and an unshakeable conviction that the truth of God's sovereignty in salvation is the most important thing the modern church has forgotten — and that someone needs to say it plainly, beautifully, and without apology.

"What do you have that you did not receive? And if you did receive it, why do you boast as though you did not?"

1 CORINTHIANS 4:7

Where the Money Goes

Every dollar goes directly into keeping this site alive and growing: hosting costs, domain fees, development tools, and the infrastructure that lets us publish content that reaches people across the globe. There is no overhead. There is no salary. There is no marketing budget — because the content is the marketing.

Give

If this site has helped you see God's grace more clearly — if a page here played even a small part in your journey toward resting in His sovereignty — consider giving back so we can reach the next person.

Give via PayPal

No amount is too small. No amount is expected. Grace is free — and so is this site. But if God moves you to help sustain it, we are deeply grateful.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

2 CORINTHIANS 9:7

Other Ways to Help

Money isn't the only way to support this ministry. The most powerful thing you can do is share the content. Send a page to someone you've been having that conversation with. Drop a link in a group chat. If a specific article helped you, it was probably written for someone you know.

You can also write to us with your testimony, your questions, or your feedback. Hearing how God is using this site to open eyes is the fuel that keeps us building.

And above all: pray. Pray that the Spirit uses these pages to find His people and bring them home. That is the only thing that makes any of this work.
