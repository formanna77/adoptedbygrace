# THE 13-PHASE STRATEGIC SESSION

### Phase 1: ANALYTICS DEEP READ
Read ANALYTICS.md thoroughly. Extract:
- Top performing categories (double down)
- Top performing individual pages (study what works)
- Underperforming categories (needs intervention)
- Week-over-week trends (growing? shrinking? plateauing?)
- Traffic ratios by category
- Search intent patterns — what are people actually Googling?
- Entry points vs. depth metrics (are readers clicking into the site or bouncing?)

### Phase 2: COMPREHENSIVE SITE CENSUS
Count every page by category. Document to CONTENT-STRATEGY-2026.md:
- question-*.html, objection-*.html, demolition-*.html
- devotional-*.html, analogy-*.html, story-*.html (with subcategories: children, humor, dark, tender, thought experiment)
- systematic-*.html, theologian-*.html, history-*.html, ot-*.html
- secular-*.html, psychology-*.html, pastoral-*.html, compare-*.html, creed-*.html
- 5 healing hubs: anxious-mind-*.html, broken-mirror-*.html, open-wound-*.html, invisible-wall-*.html, shattered-lens-*.html
- Hub pages, infrastructure pages (count separately)

### Phase 3: QUALITY SCORING SYSTEM (Define Once)
Read 10-12 pages per session (rotate each session — different pages every time). Score each 1-10 on:

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Theological Precision** | Greek/Hebrew accuracy, argument rigor, Scripture density |
| **Emotional Power** | Hook strength, conclusion impact, pastoral moments that move the reader |
| **Literary Quality** | Lewis/Buechner/Spurgeon standard or generic Christian blog? |
| **SEO Readiness** | Title targeting, meta description, structured data, keyword strategy |
| **Cross-Referencing** | Internal links (minimum 8-12), bidirectional connections |
| **Force Multiplier Density** | How many of 8 weapons deployed? (analogies, secular evidence, psychology, storytelling, etc.) |
| **Unique Contribution** | Does this page say something NO other site says? |
| **NIV Compliance** | Uses NIV translation only, not ESV/NASB/LSB |
| **Pastoral Warmth** | Reader feels loved by God, not beaten by doctrine |

Calculate average score per category. Log in CONTENT-STRATEGY-2026.md. Identify standout pages for Enhancer to study.

### Phase 4: GAP ANALYSIS
Compare census against the ideal site:
- Which TIER 1 categories (Questions, Objections, Psychology, Demolition) are underleveraged?
- Are all 5 healing hubs properly populated? (50 articles planned; how many exist?)
- Are story subcategories balanced?
- Are the 4 Book-of-the-Bible walkthrough series started? (Romans, John, Ephesians, Genesis)
- Is the Hymn Analysis series started?
- Do audience landing pages exist? (/for-skeptics, /for-pastors, /for-parents, /for-new-believers, /for-students)
- Are there enough interactive experiences? (quizzes, choose-your-path, debates)
- What's the ratio of "head" content (doctrine) to "heart" content (devotionals, stories, healing)?

Write specific page recommendations to CONTENT-STRATEGY-2026.md.

### Phase 5: SEO STRATEGY
- Identify top 20 keywords the site should rank for
- Check which pages target those keywords
- Identify keyword gaps (high-value keywords with no dedicated page)
- Review title tags of underperforming categories
- Propose title rewrites that match search intent
- Identify internal linking opportunities between high-traffic and low-traffic pages
- Review meta descriptions for top 30 pages
- Check for keyword cannibalization (multiple pages targeting same keyword)

### Phase 6: COMPETITOR ANALYSIS
Consider what Reformed/sovereignty-focused sites offer (Desiring God, Ligonier, CARM, Got Questions, Monergism, Reformed Wiki):
- What do they have that we don't?
- What do WE have that THEY don't? (secular evidence, psychology series, healing hubs, stories, humor, interactive experiences)
- Where can we create content that NO other site has?

### Phase 7: IDEATION PROTOCOL (20% of context window minimum)
1. **New Content Concepts** — 10 page ideas (specify creator: Exegete, Storyteller, Shepherd, or Bridge)
   - At least 3 from categories with fewer than 5 pages

### Phase 10: CONVERSION FUNNEL ANALYSIS
**Traffic volume is only half the story. The other half is reader BEHAVIOR.**

Analyze:

3. **BOUNCE INDICATORS** — Pages with high traffic but no outbound clicks = dead ends. Flag for linking queue.
4. **JOURNEY COMPLETION** — Are readers reaching Crown Jewel pages from other entry points?
5. **RIGHT ARM RATIO** — For every demolition/question page hit, how many also hit devotional/healing? Target: 40%+.
6. **AUDIENCE PAGE PERFORMANCE** — Track performance of audience landing pages once created.

Ensure DIVERSITY of approach:
- Head content (doctrine, systematic theology, questions)

- Bridge content (secular evidence, psychology, analogies)

### Traffic-Driven Content Strategy
- Questions/Objections = #1 traffic driver → MAXIMIZE
- Psychology = highest per-page ratio → EXPAND aggressively
- Hub pages = 7 of top 15 → OPTIMIZE every hub
- Underperforming categories need TITLE SURGERY, not more pages

### The Priority Matrix
| | High Search Demand | Low Search Demand |
|---|---|---|
| **Category Gap** (few pages) | BUILD IMMEDIATELY | Build when available |
| **Category Full** (many pages) | Optimize existing | Lowest priority |

### The "10X Test"
For every strategic decision, ask: "Will this make the site 10X better at reaching someone God is drawing?" If not, deprioritize it.

### The Content Flywheel
Create brilliant content → Optimize for search → People find it → They read more pages → They share it → More traffic → Analytics show what works → Create MORE of that
