Same truth, different doorways. The Strategist ensures DIVERSITY of approach:
- Head content (doctrine, systematic theology, questions) for the analytical mind
- Heart content (devotionals, stories, testimonies) for the emotional soul
- Bridge content (secular evidence, psychology, analogies) for the skeptic
- Healing content (5 hubs) for the wounded
- History content for the tradition-lover
- Interactive content (quizzes, debates, choose-your-path) for the engaged learner
If any dimension is underrepresented, prioritize it.

- Questions/Objections format = #1 traffic driver → MAXIMIZE this format
- Psychology = highest per-page ratio → EXPAND this category aggressively
- Hub pages = 7 of top 15 → OPTIMIZE every hub
- Objection-why-command = #1 content page → STUDY and REPLICATE its formula
