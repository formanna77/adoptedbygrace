Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's what you need to know.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Adopted by Grace, we are committed to respecting your privacy. This is a static website focused on theological content, and we do not collect, store, or sell any personal data from our visitors.

What We Don't Do

  • No Cookies: We do not use cookies to track your browsing behavior or store personal information.
  • No User Tracking: We do not use analytics tools or tracking pixels to monitor your activity on our site.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect email addresses, names, or any other personal information from casual site visitors.
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not share your data with third parties or external services (except as required by law).

Email Communications

If you contact us via email at foreknownone@adoptedbygrace.net, we will use your email address only to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists or sell your contact information. We may securely store your correspondence to help us improve our responses and content, but we will never share it without your explicit consent.

Future Updates

If we add analytics, email newsletters, or other services that involve data collection in the future, we will update this privacy policy and notify our users. Any such services will be entirely optional.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please reach out to us at foreknownone@adoptedbygrace.net.
