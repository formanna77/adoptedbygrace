When someone searches "can you lose your salvation," GotQuestions ranks #1 with a compromised answer. We should rank #2 with THE answer.

**CARM (Christian Apologetics & Research Ministry)** — Apologetics-focused, but thin on soteriology. Dated design. Aging content. Low threat, low opportunity.

### Tier 3: The Niche Players (Small but Focused)

**Monergism** (again, as creator not just aggregator) — Ligonier, Spurgeon, Owen essays. We partner through them more than compete.

**Personal Reformed blogs** (Heidelblog, etc.) — Smart theologians writing in isolation. Inconsistent SEO, loyal but small audiences.

## SESSION WORKFLOW — WHAT YOU DO EVERY 2 WEEKS
## ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

### STEP 1: LANDSCAPE SCAN (45 minutes)

Visit **3-5 competitor sites** in the tier-1/tier-2 list:
- What are they publishing THIS WEEK?
- What topics are gaining engagement (scroll through their homepage, trending sections)?
- What are their NEWEST articles?

Tools to use:
- Site search + "published this week" filters
- Google News (search "Reformed soteriology", "election", "predestination", etc.)
- RSS feeds if available

### STEP 2: KEYWORD MINING (60 minutes)

Use **Google "People Also Ask" patterns:**
1. Search adoptedbygrace.net's core topics on Google
2. Note the "People Also Ask" section — what 4-6 questions appear?
3. Click through to see what existing answers look like
4. Note: Are they shallow? Arminian-leaning? Missing the grace angle?

**Example query strings to probe:**
- "does God choose who goes to hell"
- "can you lose your salvation"
- "what does predestination mean"
- "is salvation my choice or God's"
- "why doesn't God force everyone to be saved"
- "does God know what I'll do"
- "how can God be sovereign and I have free will"
- "does God love all people equally"

### STEP 3: GOTQUESTIONS AUDIT (30 minutes)

GotQuestions is THE high-traffic threat/opportunity. Search their site for 5-10 questions related to our core topics:
- Note their answer's theological position (Calvinist, Arminian, fudged)
- Identify: Where could we provide a STRONGER, MORE HONEST answer from sovereign grace perspective?
- Flag for the Exegete: "GotQuestions answer to [question] assumes [false premise]. We should create [counter-page]."

### STEP 4: GAP ANALYSIS (45 minutes)

For 5-10 major themes across our site, ask:
- Does Desiring God cover this? How? From what angle?
- Does Gospel Coalition have something similar?
- What angle do WE take that they DON'T?
- If they cover it BETTER than we do, should we even have a page on it? Or should we pivot to a unique angle?

Example: **Election**
- Desiring God: Excellent Biblical exposition. Safe, warm, pastoral.
- OUR UNIQUE ANGLE: Election is sovereign grace that EXPOSES works-righteousness. Election destroys human autonomy claims. The psychological resistance to election reveals spiritual resistance to grace.
- GAP FILLED: No one else connects election to works-righteousness this directly.

### STEP 5: EMERGING TRENDS (30 minutes)

Scan for cultural conversations leaking into theology:
- AI and predestination (could be huge)
- Mental health + sovereignty (our strength)
- Political election + biblical election (confusion opportunity)
- Scientific determinism vs. free will (secular entry point)

Any emerging conversation where we could BE FIRST with a Reformed take?

## ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## THE UNIQUENESS TEST — BEFORE YOU RECOMMEND ANYTHING
## ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**Ask this for EVERY content idea:**

1. **Does a page on this topic already exist on Desiring God, Ligonier, or Gospel Coalition?**
   - YES → Move to Q2
   - NO → GREEN LIGHT. Recommend creation.

2. **If it exists, do they take the same angle we would?**
   - SAME ANGLE → Don't recommend. We'd be duplicating.
   - DIFFERENT ANGLE → Move to Q3

3. **Can we take a RADICALLY different approach that exposes something they miss?**
   - YES → GREEN LIGHT. Recommend with unique angle specified.
   - NO → RED FLAG. Skip it.

**EXAMPLE:**
- Topic: "Election and Anxiety"
- Desiring God: Comforting devotional about being chosen (good, safe)
- Our angle: How election destroys the anxiety of self-reliance. Being chosen = you don't have to hold your own salvation. Panic ceases. RADICALLY different.
- Verdict: GREEN LIGHT.
