## SESSION WORKFLOW
## ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

### STEP 1: LANDSCAPE SCAN (45 min)

Visit 3-5 competitor sites from Tier 1/2 above. Ask:
- What are they publishing THIS WEEK?
- What topics are gaining engagement (homepage trending, recent posts)?
- What are their newest articles?

**Tools:** Site search + "published this week" filters, Google News (search "Reformed soteriology", "election", "predestination"), RSS feeds.

### STEP 2: KEYWORD MINING (60 min)

Use Google "People Also Ask" to uncover unmet demand.

**Workflow:**
1. Search adoptedbygrace.net's core topics on Google
2. Note the "People Also Ask" section (4-6 questions)
3. Click through; assess existing answers (shallow? Arminian-leaning? Missing grace angle?)

**Probe these query strings:**
- "does God choose who goes to hell"
- "can you lose your salvation"
- "what does predestination mean"
- "is salvation my choice or God's"
- "why doesn't God force everyone to be saved"
- "how can God be sovereign and I have free will"
- "does God know what I'll do before I do it"

### STEP 3: GOTQUESTIONS AUDIT (30 min)

GotQuestions is THE high-traffic threat. Search their site for 5-10 soteriology questions:
- What's their theological position (Calvinist, Arminian, fudged)?
- Where could we provide a STRONGER, MORE HONEST answer?
- Flag for the Exegete: "GotQuestions answer to [question] assumes [false premise]. We should create [counter-page]."

### STEP 4: GAP ANALYSIS (45 min)

For 5-10 major themes across our site, ask:
- Does Desiring God cover this? From what angle?
- Does Gospel Coalition have something similar?
- What angle do WE take that they DON'T?
- If they cover it BETTER than we do, should we even have a page on it?

**Example:** Election
- Desiring God: Excellent Biblical exposition. Safe, warm, pastoral.
- Our angle: Election is sovereign grace that EXPOSES works-righteousness. Election destroys human autonomy claims. (See Crown Jewel in `/.claude/CLAUDE.md`.)
- Gap filled: No one else connects election to works-righteousness this directly.

### STEP 5: EMERGING TRENDS (30 min)

Scan for cultural conversations leaking into theology:
- AI and predestination
- Mental health + sovereignty (our strength)
- Political election + biblical election (confusion opportunity)
- Scientific determinism vs. free will (secular entry point)

Any emerging conversation where we could BE FIRST with a Reformed take?

### STEP 6: UPDATE SCOUT-REPORT.md (30 min)

Create or update `/SCOUT-REPORT.md` using the format defined below.

## ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
## THE UNIQUENESS TEST
## ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**Ask this for EVERY content idea:**

1. **Does a page on this topic already exist on Desiring God, Ligonier, or Gospel Coalition?**
   - YES → Move to Q2
   - NO → GREEN LIGHT. Recommend creation.

2. **If it exists, do they take the same angle we would?**
   - SAME ANGLE → Don't recommend. We'd be duplicating.
   - DIFFERENT ANGLE → Move to Q3

3. **Can we take a RADICALLY different approach that exposes something they miss?**
   - YES → GREEN LIGHT. Recommend with unique angle.
   - NO → RED FLAG. Skip it.

**Example:** "Election and Anxiety"
- Desiring God: Comforting devotional about being chosen (good, safe)
- Our angle: How election destroys the anxiety of self-reliance. Being chosen = you don't have to hold your own salvation. Panic ceases.
- Verdict: GREEN LIGHT.
