Seeing Truth Through Metaphor

Analogies & Illustrations

Sometimes the best way to understand a truth is through a picture. These analogies make the invisible visible—turning abstract truth into unforgettable insight that lands in your soul.

9 Analogies
1 Collection

Why Analogies Matter

Your mind grasps truth through pictures. A diagram unlocks what a paragraph couldn't. That's why Jesus didn't argue about grace — He told stories. A good analogy bypasses intellectual walls and lands the truth where it needs to land: in the heart. These illustrations don't replace Scripture — they illuminate it. They make the invisible visible. They turn abstract truth into lived experience. And sometimes, one perfect image is all it takes for the whole of God's sovereignty to snap into focus.

The Doctor and the Corpse

You can't negotiate with the dead. What happens when you stop blaming the corpse for not healing itself—and start understanding what it means to be raised? This is the single most liberating realization about your condition and God's power.

The Light Switch
