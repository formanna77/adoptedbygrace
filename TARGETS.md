## Priority Key

- **CRITICAL**: Extremely high traffic, widely shared, or serves as a foundational reference for anti-Reformed arguments
- **HIGH**: Significant readership, frequently cited in debates
- **MEDIUM**: Notable but smaller audience, or duplicates arguments already addressed elsewhere

## Notes

- Focus on CRITICAL and HIGH targets first
- Each demolition must link back to the original article for verification
- Every Scripture citation in the target must be addressed with proper exegesis
- Expose texts the target avoids (Romans 9, Ephesians 1, John 6, John 10, Acts 13:48, 2 Tim 1:9)
