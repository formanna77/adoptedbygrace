Systematic Theology

The Whole Counsel of God

Theology is not a collection of disconnected truths. It is one seamless garment, woven by one God, revealed in one Word, centered on one Savior. Every doctrine touches every other doctrine — and together they display the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ.

"Oh, the depth of the riches and wisdom and knowledge of God! How unsearchable are his judgments and how inscrutable his ways! For who has known the mind of the Lord, or who has been his counselor? Or who has given a gift to him that he might be repaid? For from him and through him and to him are all things. To him be glory forever. Amen." — Romans 11:33-36 (ESV)

Soteriology

The Doctrine of Salvation

How It All Connects

Systematic theology is not a filing cabinet. It is a living system in which every truth depends on and illuminates every other truth. Here is the architecture of Reformed soteriology.

God Decrees
Before the foundation of the world, the Triune God freely and unchangeably ordains whatsoever comes to pass — including the salvation of His elect.
→ Read: Divine Decrees
The Covenant Unfolds
God executes His decree through covenants — one plan of grace, progressively revealed from Eden to Calvary, mediated by Christ.
→ Read: Covenant Theology
Salvation Applied
The Spirit applies redemption in an unbreakable order — calling, regeneration, faith, justification, sanctification, glorification. No link can fail.
→ Read: Ordo Salutis
Both True, Both Real
God's sovereign decree and human responsibility are not in tension. Compatibilism shows how Scripture holds both together — and why it must.
→ Read: Compatibilism
