Systematic Theology

The Whole Counsel of God

Theology is not a collection of disconnected truths. It is one seamless garment, woven by one God, revealed in one Word, centered on one Savior. Every doctrine touches every other doctrine — and together they display the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ.

"Oh, the depth of the riches and wisdom and knowledge of God! How unsearchable are his judgments and how inscrutable his ways! For who has known the mind of the Lord, or who has been his counselor? Or who has given a gift to him that he might be repaid? For from him and through him and to him are all things. To him be glory forever. Amen." — Romans 11:33-36 (ESV)

Foundations

God and His Eternal Purpose

Before anything else, there is God. Before creation, before time, before the fall — there is the Triune God and His eternal decree. Everything that follows in theology flows from who God is and what He has purposed.

⚡
The Divine Decrees
God's eternal, holy, wise, and free purpose — whereby He foreordains whatsoever comes to pass. From predestination to providence, from the cross to the sparrow.
Eph 1:11 · Isa 46:9-10 · Acts 2:23

Soteriology

The Doctrine of Salvation

How are sinners saved? Not by anything in themselves — but by the sovereign, effectual, irresistible grace of God applied through the work of the Holy Spirit. The ordo salutis traces the unbreakable chain from election to glorification.

🔗
The Ordo Salutis — The Order of Salvation
From election to glorification, every link in the golden chain. Election, calling, regeneration, faith, justification, adoption, sanctification, perseverance, glorification — all forged by sovereign grace.
Rom 8:29-30 · Eph 1:3-14 · Titus 3:5
Live
✝️
Christology — The Person and Work of Christ
The two natures, the three offices, active and passive obedience, definite atonement. Who is the Christ, and what did He accomplish?
Col 1:15-20 · Heb 1:1-3 · John 10:11
Live
🕊️
Pneumatology — The Work of the Holy Spirit
Regeneration, illumination, indwelling, sealing, sanctification. The Spirit applies what the Son accomplished and what the Father decreed.

Hamartiology — The Doctrine of Sin
Original sin, total depravity, the fall of Adam, and imputation. Why salvation must be entirely of grace — because the problem is total.

Theology & Philosophy

Hard Questions, Biblical Answers

Reformed theology does not shrink from the hard questions. It addresses them head-on — with Scripture, with reason, and with reverence.

⚖️
Compatibilism — Sovereignty and Responsibility
Scripture never asks you to choose between God's absolute sovereignty and genuine human responsibility. It affirms both — without apology, without contradiction.
Acts 2:23 · Gen 50:20 · Phil 2:12-13

How It All Connects

Systematic theology is not a filing cabinet. It is a living system in which every truth depends on and illuminates every other truth. Here is the architecture of Reformed soteriology.

God Decrees
Before the foundation of the world, the Triune God freely and unchangeably ordains whatsoever comes to pass — including the salvation of His elect.
→ Read: Divine Decrees
The Covenant Unfolds
God executes His decree through covenants — one plan of grace, progressively revealed from Eden to Calvary, mediated by Christ.
→ Read: Covenant Theology
Salvation Applied
The Spirit applies redemption in an unbreakable order — calling, regeneration, faith, justification, sanctification, glorification. No link can fail.
→ Read: Ordo Salutis
Both True, Both Real
God's sovereign decree and human responsibility are not in tension. Compatibilism shows how Scripture holds both together — and why it must.
→ Read: Compatibilism
