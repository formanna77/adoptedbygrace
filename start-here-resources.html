Objections
What About Free Will and Fairness?

Recommended Reading

Freedom of the Will
Jonathan Edwards
The greatest philosophical treatise on the will ever written. Challenging but devastating. Edwards systematically shows that "libertarian free will" is incoherent.
The Bondage of the Will
Martin Luther
Luther's response to Erasmus — the book Luther himself considered his most important work. Passionate, biblical, and prophetic.
Willing to Believe
R.C. Sproul
A history of the free will debate from Augustine to modern Arminianism. Gives you context for why this matters and where objections come from.
