Total Depravity
DEPRAVITAS TOTALIS
Sin hasn't merely wounded humanity — it has killed us. Every faculty of the soul is corrupted. The mind is darkened, the will is enslaved, the heart is stone. No one seeks God. No one can. Apart from sovereign intervention, every human being is a spiritual corpse.

“The natural person does not accept the things of the Spirit of God, for they are folly to him, and he is not able to understand them.”

1 Corinthians 2:14
Explore the evidence →
Unconditional Election
ELECTIO GRATUITA
God does not choose based on foreseen faith, merit, or good works. Before the foundation of the world, He set His love on specific individuals — not because they would choose Him, but because He chose them. Election is the Father's free, sovereign, undeserved love.

“He chose us in Him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and blameless before Him. In love He predestined us for adoption.”

Ephesians 1:4-5
Explore the evidence →
Limited Atonement
REDEMPTIO PARTICULARIS
Christ did not die to make salvation possible — He died to make it certain. The cross actually accomplished redemption for God's elect. Jesus didn't just open a door; He carried His sheep through it. Every person for whom Christ died will be saved.

“I lay down my life for the sheep… You do not believe because you are not among my sheep.”

John 10:15, 26
Explore the evidence →
Irresistible Grace
GRATIA EFFICAX
When God calls His elect, He doesn't merely invite — He resurrects. The same power that called Lazarus from the tomb calls sinners from spiritual death. God's grace doesn't just make salvation available; it makes the unwilling willing. It transforms the heart.

“All that the Father gives me will come to me, and whoever comes to me I will never cast out.”

John 6:37
Explore the evidence →
Perseverance of the Saints
PERSEVERANTIA SANCTORUM
Those whom God has chosen, redeemed, and called He will keep. Not one will be lost. Salvation is not a tightrope walk where the believer might fall — it is an unbreakable chain forged in eternity. He who began a good work in you will bring it to completion.

“I give them eternal life, and they will never perish, and no one will snatch them out of my hand.”

John 10:28
