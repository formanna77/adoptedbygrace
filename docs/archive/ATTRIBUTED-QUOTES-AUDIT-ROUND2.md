### connections.html Fixes (3 quotes)

**Sproul — Line 1083**
- **Before:** `"God owes sinners no mercy — yet He gives it to some. This is grace."`
- **After:** `Sproul taught that God owes sinners no mercy—yet He gives it to some. This is grace in its essence.`

**Owen — Line 1092**
- **Before:** `"Sinners cannot obey the gospel any more than the law, apart from sovereign grace."`

**Pink — Line 1155**
- **Before:** `"We are not saved because we believe; we believe because we are saved."`

**MacArthur — Line 1164**
- **Before:** `"Grace is not a reward for faith; faith is the result of grace."`
- **After:** `MacArthur emphasizes that grace is not a reward for faith; rather, faith itself is the fruit of prior divine grace.`

### analogy-chess-grandmaster.html Fixes (1 quote)

**Line 357 — Sproul**
