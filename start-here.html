A Guided Journey

New to This? Start Here.

You've heard the terms — predestination, election, sovereign grace. Maybe they trouble you. Maybe they fascinate you. Either way, this journey is for you.

"Come now, let us reason together, says the LORD."

Isaiah 1:18

We've designed a five-phase pathway to walk you through what the Bible actually teaches about salvation — from your very first question to the deepest convictions of your heart. Each phase builds on the last, but you can jump in wherever you are.

This isn't about winning an argument. It's about falling in love with a God who loved you first — before you existed, before the foundation of the world.
