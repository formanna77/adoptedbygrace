A Guided Journey

New to This? Start Here.

You've heard the words — predestination, election, sovereign grace. Maybe they make your stomach tighten. Maybe they make your heart race. Either way, something brought you here. We don't think that was an accident.

"Come now, let us reason together, says the LORD."

Isaiah 1:18

What follows is a five-phase pathway through the Bible's own teaching on salvation — from your very first question to the deepest convictions of your heart. Each phase builds on the last, but you're free to jump in wherever you are. There's no test at the end. No pressure. Just Scripture, honestly examined.

This isn't about winning a theological debate. It's about something far more dangerous and far more beautiful: discovering that you are loved by a God who loved you first — before you drew breath, before the foundations of the world were laid, before time itself began its ticking.

This Journey Is For You If…

✹

You're Curious

You've heard people talk about predestination and something in you wants to know: is this real? Does the Bible actually teach this? You're not looking for a fight — you're looking for the truth. That's exactly where the best journeys begin.

✶

You're Skeptical

You've been told these doctrines make God a monster. Fair enough — we'd reject that God too. But what if the version you were taught to reject isn't what Scripture actually says? Come with your objections loaded. We welcome them.

✨

You're Seeking Deeper

You already believe in grace, but you sense there's an ocean beneath the surface you haven't yet explored. You want to understand why these truths have sustained martyrs at the stake and comforted saints on their deathbeds for two millennia.

Study This Journey Together

These truths were never meant to be explored alone. Walking through them with others — wrestling, questioning, marveling together — transforms intellectual assent into something that rewires your soul.
