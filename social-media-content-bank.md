**Post 5 (Morning):**

Only one of those makes God's choice actually mean something.

Scripture is devastatingly clear on which one.

→ https://adoptedbygrace.net/compare-predestination-foreknowledge.html

## POSTING NOTES

**Best times to post (general faith/theology audience):**
- X/Twitter: 8-9am and 7-9pm (engagement peaks)
- Instagram: 11am-1pm and 7-8pm
- Threads: Similar to Instagram, slightly later evening

**Hashtag strategy (Instagram only):**
- Mix broad (#theology #biblestudy #grace) with niche (#reformed #sovereignty #election)
- 8-12 hashtags per post, never 30 (looks spammy)
- Threads: skip hashtags entirely, conversational tone only

**Cross-posting rules:**
- X content can post to Threads nearly verbatim (adjust length)
- Instagram captions are LONGER and more emotional — don't just copy X posts
- Never post identical content across all 3 simultaneously — stagger by 2-4 hours minimum

**Link placement:**
- X: Link in tweet body (it's expected)
- Instagram: "Link in bio" + full URL in caption (won't be clickable but establishes the path). Use Linktree or similar for bio link rotation
- Threads: Link in post body (clickable)

**Bio recommendation (all platforms):**
