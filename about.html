For the One Who Can't Un-See It

This site exists because salvation is not a transaction you completed — it is a rescue you survived. Every page here is built on a single conviction: that God saves His people from first to last, that grace is not an offer extended to the willing but a resurrection performed on the dead, and that the moment you stop taking credit for your own rescue is the moment you finally see how loved you are. We write for the searcher at 2am, the believer who senses something doesn't add up, the wounded soul who has been told their salvation depends on the strength of their own grip. We write to tell them the truth that will set them free: the grip was never yours. It was His. And He has never let go.

How We Build Every Page

01

Original Languages First

We parse the Greek and Hebrew — tense, voice, mood. English translations are gentle with you. The original languages are not. We let them speak.

02

Arguments Nobody Else Is Making

You've heard the standard arguments. We make the ones nobody else is making — from the grammar of doxology, from the internal contradictions of rival views, from the inferences the text demands and most commentaries dodge.

03

Steel-Man Every Objection

We never fight straw men. Every objection gets its strongest form — the version hardest to answer, the one your smartest friend would use. Then we let the text respond.

04

Follow the Text Wherever It Leads

We did not start with a system and go looking for proof texts. We started with the text and followed it. It led somewhere most churches won't go. We went anyway.

05

Give God All the Glory

Every page on this site exists for one purpose: to make God bigger in your eyes and your contribution to your salvation smaller — until it disappears entirely, and all that remains is grace. Soli Deo Gloria.
