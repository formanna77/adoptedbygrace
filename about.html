Why This Site Exists For the One Who Can't Un-See It

Here's to the ones who couldn't leave it alone. The restless. The wrestlers. The 2am searchers who opened Romans 9 and felt the ground disappear. They fought it. They argued with God and lost. They ran — some for years, some for decades. But grace is a hunter, and it does not miss. It tracked them through every objection, every sleepless night, every pew where something didn't add up — until the day they stopped running and whispered what they had always known: I didn't choose God. He chose me. The world calls them fanatics. The church calls them divisive. We call them found. Because the people finally honest enough to stop taking credit for their own rescue are the ones who discover they were held all along.

How We Build Every Page

01 Original Languages First

We parse the Greek and Hebrew — tense, voice, mood. English translations are gentle with you. The original languages are not. We let them speak.

02 Arguments Nobody Else Is Making

You've heard the standard arguments. We make the ones nobody else is making — from the grammar of doxology, from the internal contradictions of rival views, from the inferences the text demands and most commentaries dodge.

03 Steel-Man Every Objection

We never fight straw men. Every objection gets its strongest form — the version hardest to answer, the one your smartest friend would use. Then we let the text respond.

04 Follow the Text Wherever It Leads

We did not start with a system and go looking for proof texts. We started with the text and followed it. It led somewhere most churches won't go. We went anyway.

05 Give God All the Glory

Every page on this site exists for one purpose: to make God bigger in your eyes and your contribution to your salvation smaller — until it disappears entirely, and all that remains is grace. Soli Deo Gloria.
